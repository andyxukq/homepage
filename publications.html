Publications by Kaiqiang Xu

My research focuses on improving the efficiency and accessibility of machine learning systems and AI computing infrastructure. I work on designing new abstractions, parallelization strategies, and scheduling algorithms that exploit the unique characteristics of machine learning workloads and large-scale clusters.
