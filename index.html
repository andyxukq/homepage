Research

My research focuses on designing efficient and accessible systems for machine learning. I study the system foundations of large-scale AI — from new system abstractions to scheduling algorithms and parallelization strategies that enable more accessible and efficient AI computing infrastructure.

My work has been published in top-tier conferences across computer networks, operating systems, computer architecture, and databases, including NSDI, OSDI, ASPLOS, and SIGMOD.
