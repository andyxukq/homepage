Kaiqiang Xu 徐凯强

PhD in Computer Science (Expected 2025), HKUST
My work seeks the convergence of technology and creativity. During my PhD at HKUST, supervised by Prof. Kai Chen, I design efficient and accessible systems for machine learning, with publications in top-tier conferences. Earlier, as COO of Vincross Robotics (2016-2020), I led a global team to create a world-recognized all-terrain robot and was interviewed by the Wall Street Journal.

Research

My research focuses on advancing the efficiency and accessibility of machine learning systems by introducing novel abstractions, parallelization strategies, and scheduling algorithms that leverage the unique characteristics of large-scale GPU clusters. I have published in top-tier conferences across the fields of networked systems, architecture design, and data management.
